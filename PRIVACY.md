# Privacy Policy

FrenchVerbQuiz does not collect, store, or share personal data.

## Data Collection
This app does not require account creation and does not collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, or payment information.

## Quiz Data
Quiz progress and answers are used only within the app for the user experience and are not linked to your identity.

## Remote Content
The app may load a remote JSON file to update verb content. This does not collect personal user data.

## Third-Party Services
FrenchVerbQuiz does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools.
